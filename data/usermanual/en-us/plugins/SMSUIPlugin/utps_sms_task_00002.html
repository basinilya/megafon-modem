Creating and Sending a Text Message

With the MegaFon | Modem installed on your PC, you can send text messages to mobile phones or other terminal devices that support the SMS function.

Context

NOTE:

You can group send a text message. Each phone number should be separated by ;.

Procedure

  1. Choose Service > SMS-Client.
  2. Click .
  3. Enter the recipient number in one of the following ways:
    • Click Add Contact and then click to select the phone number in the pop-up window.
    • Enter the phone number of the recipient directly.
  4. Enter the message text.
  5. After editing a text message, you can choose from the following options:
    • Click Send: to send the text message.
    • Click Save: to save the text message as a draft.
    • Click Close: to close the text message under editing. If you click Yes in the displayed prompt window, the text message is saved in Draft.
