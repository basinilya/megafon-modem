Connecting to the Internet

The terminal device must connect to the Internet before you can surf the Internet.

Context

  • When the icon is displayed on the MegaFon | Modem interface, it indicates that the network connection is established.
  • When the icon is displayed on the MegaFon | Modem interface, it indicates that the network connection is disconnected.
NOTE:

After you set the network connection parameters correctly, do as follows to connect to the network.

Procedure

  1. Click the Connect button to connect the network.
  2. Launch the browser on the personal computer (PC) to surf the Internet.
